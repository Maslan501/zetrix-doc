# Dashboard

#### **Purpose**

The Dashboard Home is the **main control center** of the Zetrix Multisig DApp. It provides a consolidated overview of every multisignature account associated with your wallet, allowing you to quickly understand your operational responsibilities and navigate between different joint accounts with ease.

It is designed to support:

* Daily operational monitoring
* Quick decision-making
* Efficient account switching
* Centralized visibility across multiple shared wallets
